About Us

We are a leading biomedical research startup in Kazakhstan specializing in peptide-based intravascular therapies. Our team of scientists and medical researchers are dedicated to advancing peptide treatments that address a wide range of complex medical conditions.

Our mission is to harness the power of peptide science to develop innovative solutions for improving patient outcomes, while ensuring the highest levels of safety and efficacy.

Our Services

  • Peptide Therapy Development: Customized peptide treatments designed to target specific diseases and conditions, offering patients a new frontier in medical care.
  • Biomedical Research: Cutting-edge research aimed at understanding the therapeutic potential of peptides in treating cancer, neurological diseases, and autoimmune disorders.
  • Clinical Trials: Advanced clinical trials focusing on peptide-based therapies, ensuring treatments meet rigorous standards before reaching the market.
  • Consulting Services: Expert guidance for healthcare professionals and companies interested in integrating peptide treatments into their practices and products.
  • Personalized Medicine: Leveraging the latest advancements in peptides to create personalized treatment plans for patients based on their unique genetic profiles.

Our Products

Our peptide-based products are designed to transform healthcare through targeted therapies addressing various medical conditions.

  • Peptide Cancer Treatment: Targeted therapies aimed at reducing tumor size and preventing cancer cell growth.
  • Neuroprotective Peptides: Designed to promote neurogenesis and alleviate symptoms of neurodegenerative diseases.
  • Immune System Modulation: Formulations that regulate the immune system, aiding in fighting infections and preventing autoimmune disorders.
